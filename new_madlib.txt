I, the cool and bright Bob, have baked Mary's energetic sister and plan to steal her dumb

frogs! What are a bear and backpacking hamster to do? Before you can help Glenda, you'll have to collect

the angry cookies and red clouds that open up the 30 worlds connected to Ernie's Lair.

There are 7 cups and 48 hammers in the game, along with hundreds of other goodies for you to find.
